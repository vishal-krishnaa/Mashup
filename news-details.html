City council approves major transit expansion across downtown districts

By Maya Carter • 21 July 2026

City officials announced a wide-reaching transit upgrade plan designed to reduce congestion and improve mobility for residents and visitors throughout the downtown core. The proposal includes new bus lanes, expanded rail access, and safer pedestrian routes linking key neighborhoods.

Supporters say the initiative will help families save time, strengthen local businesses, and make public transportation more sustainable. Community leaders also welcomed the additional investment in accessibility and public spaces.

Construction is expected to begin next year, with phased improvements scheduled over the next several years. City planners say the project will be monitored closely to ensure transparency and measurable benefits for citizens.
